Ericeira Market Lens

A data-driven briefing on the Ericeira real estate market

This dashboard aggregates and visualises public listing data from Idealista for properties in the Ericeira area. It is designed to give a structured, at-a-glance overview of what is currently on the market, prices, surfaces, typologies, agencies, and space efficiency.

Disclaimer: This is not a valuation tool, a price forecast, or financial advice. All data comes from public listings and reflects asking prices, not transaction prices. Listings may contain errors, duplicates, or outdated information.

An opaque market

The Portuguese real estate market, and Ericeira in particular, is notoriously opaque. Transaction prices are rarely published, agency commissions vary widely, and reliable comparable data is hard to find. Buyers, sellers, and even professionals often navigate with limited visibility.

This project aims to reduce that asymmetry by making the publicly available listing data more accessible, structured, and comparable. It does not replace professional advice, but it provides a starting point for informed conversations.

For whom?

  • Buyers looking to understand the current supply and relative pricing
  • Sellers wanting to position their property against the market
  • Agents & advisors who need a quick market snapshot
  • Curious minds interested in Ericeira real estate dynamics

The focus is on relative positioning, not absolute truth.
See where a property stands compared to others, not what it is "worth".
