I. The current imbroglio

People's need for long-term investment has never been higher, but the offer remains very limited and expensive.

EUROPEANS BUILD UP SAVINGS … IN CASH

Saving ratio 18% and 14% in France and Germany, and 7.5% in the US.
70% of these savings are in cash or equivalents.


During Covid , people saved 15% more than usual.

AGEING POP, INFLATION…HIGH INVESTMENT NEEDS
Ageing population, nonlinear careers and self-employment
increase the need for investing for retirement.

The rising inflation is threatening people’s saving and purchasing power.

CURRENT OFFER LIMITED & EXPENSIVE

Financial advisors?
Limited to high-net-worth individuals, expensive (advisory fee averages 4% in France), lack of transparency, tedious and tiresome process.

Bitcoin? Robinhood?
A gamble rather than an investment. High Risk of bankruptcy.

MILLENNIALS ARE COMING

Millennials are the next investors with high requirements for alternative or sustainable Investments.
