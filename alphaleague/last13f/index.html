Changes in portfolio between the two most recent quarters. Δ% weight can be misleading when options are present: adding large Put positions inflates total AUM, compressing all stock weights even with no stock trades. Δ$ (shares × price) is immune to this distortion — it directly measures conviction in dollar terms and is the preferred view.
