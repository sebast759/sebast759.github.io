Crypto Active Strategies

Get exposure to cryptocurrencies... with a plan

TAME has combined track record a suite of strategies and offer long and risk-controled exposure to a diversified portfolio of cryptocurrencies (Bitcoin, Ethereum and other liquid Altcoins).

The strategies belong to what we named the Mozaic family. They are an adaptation of a dual-momentum methodology, developed 15 years ago by JP Morgan on traditional financial markets, and that I have been investing with success in my Global Macro fund for years. This is a very powerful and versatile process to build diversified portfolio with an active protection against decline. It generates a strong performance on all diversified universe, and notably cryptos. It is here designed on the universe of 20-40 coins, the most liquid ones.

  1. Mozaic Long : Long portfolio of the best coins [w=100%]
    2. That strategy is always 100% invested on the top performing coins. (tearsheet)
  2. Mozaic Flexible-Long : Long portfolio of the best coins, with a flexible global exposure [w=0-100%]
    3. That strategy is invested on the top performing coins, providing their trend is positive. The net exposure is [0%-100%] and can decline to 0%, should the trend turn negative. (tearsheet)
  3. Mozaic Long - Short : Long / Short portfolio [+100%/-100%, gross=0-200% / net = 0-100%]
    4. That strategy is invested in the top performing coins, providing their trend is positive and short the poorly performing coins, providing their trend is negative. The net expo is always positive [0%-100%], and the gross expo is [0%-200%]. (tearsheet)
  4. Protection against Liquidity Crunch
    5. On the top of the Mozaic strategy we had an active 'liquity crunch overlay' aiming at protecting the portfolio when liquidity is drying affecting notably the alt coins (vs. BTC) (tearsheet)
  5. Overnight : Overnight strategy on Bitcoin / Ethereum / Ripple
    6. That strategy is exposed to Bitcoin / Ethereum / Ripple at certain times during the day. At least one Long position [0%-100%] is taken every day. Short exposures can be taken should the global trend is negative. (tearsheet)
The track record is net of transaction fees, assuming a 10 bp transaction cost for the Mozaic strategies (20-40 coins) and 5 bp for the overnight strategy (Bitcoin / Ethereum / Ripple)

2018-2022, Mozaic Performance

The Mozaic strategy was developed early 2022 and performance was backtested from 2018 (5 years). As of the end of 2022,
  • Sharpe ratio > 3, gross of all fees. Sharpe ratio > 2, net of 10 bp transaction fees.
  • In 2022 meltdowns, the strategy declined 13% compared to -60% for Bitcoin and -80% for a large universe of liquid cryptos. FTX collapse (November 2022) is an out of sample event included in our track record.
  • Asymetric profile: the strategy is expected to capture 60% of Bitcoin upside but only 20% of Bitcoin declines
The Overnight strategy was developed early 2023 and performance was backtested from 2019 (4 years).
