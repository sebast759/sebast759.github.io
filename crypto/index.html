Get exposure to cryptocurrencies

.... with a plan

The strategy offer access to a diversified portfolio of cryptocurrencies:

  • Takes long exposure [0%,100%] on 20-40 coins, the most liquid ones
  • Automatically selects the strongest cryptocurrencies and avoid the fragiles ones
  • Dynamically modulates leverage based on current market and liquidity risk
  • Can transitorily and moderately short some coins to increase portfolio resilience in crypto meltdowns

That strategy was developed early 2022 and performance was backtested from 2018 (5 years).
As of the end of 2022,

  • Sharpe ratio of 3 gross of all fees. Sharpe ratio of 2, net of 10 bp transaction fees.
  • Since 2018, the strategy compounded a (simulated) return of 100% per year, compared to 4% for Bitcoin and -20% for the crypto universe
  • In 2022 meltdowns, the strategy declined 13% compared to 60% for Bitcoin and 80% for our investment universe. FTX collapse (November 2022) is an out of sample event.
  • Asymetric profile: the strategy captured 60% of Bitcoin upside but only 20% of Bitcoin declines
