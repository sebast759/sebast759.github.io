The Mozaic strategy, long and protected

The strategy offers a long and protected exposure to a diversified portfolio of cryptocurrencies:

  • Takes long exposure [0%,100%] on 20-40 coins, the most liquid ones
  • Automatically selects the strongest cryptocurrencies and avoid the fragiles ones, on a daily basis
  • Dynamically modulates leverage based on current market and liquidity risk
  • Can transitorily and moderately short some coins to increase portfolio resilience notably in periods of crypto meltdowns (e.g. 2018, 2022)

That strategy was developed early 2022 and performance was backtested from 2018 (5 years).

2018-2022, Performance

As of the end of 2022,
  • Sharpe ratio > 3, gross of all fees.
    Sharpe ratio > 2, net of 10 bp transaction fees.
  • Since 2018, the strategy compounded a (simulated) return of 100% per year, compared to 4% for Bitcoin and -20% for the crypto universe
  • In 2022 meltdowns, the strategy declined 13% compared to -60% for Bitcoin and -80% for a large universe of liquid cryptos. FTX collapse (November 2022) is an out of sample event included in our track record.
  • Asymetric profile: the strategy is expected to capture 60% of Bitcoin upside but only 20% of Bitcoin declines
